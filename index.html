we take care of your healthy life

Medicare is Cambodia's first healthcare appointment booking service. Patients can use Medicare to look for a doctor or other healthcare professional and schedule appointments online.

Our website was launched with the help of 50 Cambodian health professionals with extensive experience. The goal is to help improve the Cambodian healthcare system and the lives of patients by:

  • Cambodian healthcare providers are listed onsite.
  • Providing complete transparency with regard to medical fees
  • Allowing patients to rate healthcare providers

The main advantages of SabayCare for the patients are:

  • Easy to find quickly available health professionals
  • Online reservations are available 24 hours a day, 7 days (no searching, no waiting and no hold period)
  • Mobile, tablet, and computer users will find it simple to use.

Diabetes: Avoiding Type 2 Diabetes

Diabetes develops when blood sugar levels rise above normal, and because it is a chronic condition, it can harm cells and other organs if not treated, managed, and monitored properly. Insulin is a hormone produced by pancreatic cells. It makes it easier for glucose from the blood to enter the cells and be used as a source of energy. The volume and efficiency of hormones decreases in diabetics, which is a key cause of high blood sugar levels daily.
