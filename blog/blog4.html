Mental Health Care During COVID-19

Introduction

There are 4 main happy chemical such as : dopamine; oxytocin; endorphin; serotonin.

  • Dopamine: the reward chemical
  • Oxytocin: the love hormone
  • Endorphin: the pain killer
  • Serotonin: the mode stabilizer

How to take care your mental health?

dopamine

By completing task or doing self-care activities or eating food or celebrating little wins

oxytocin

By hug family or holding hands or give complement

endorphin

By laughter exercise or watch comedy or exercising

serotonin

By mediatating or running or sun exposure or walk in nature
